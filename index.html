What is Untouchable?

At our physical location in Shibuya we offer the finest model weapons and various accessories for true enthusiasts. With our affordable prices and discount for regulars there is no doubt that this is the place you'd want to go for your needs. We offer customizations to our ranged models and are willing to purchase most "interesting" wares. Note: We are unable to handle the sale of goods online and are able to more accurately appraise what you bring in better in person. In general, you should come in to see our full stock and the newest items we have on sale (as I'm still a student and can't update the page constantly).
